# Strategy Guide — TBS #1–#100

## Directional Debit Spreads (TBS #001–#010)
Define a max-loss, directional thesis using vertical debit spreads. Best used when IV rank is moderate (<50) to avoid overpaying for extrinsic value.

## Volatility Mean Reversion (TBS #011–#020)
Trade IV reversion using straddle/strangle entries or VIX-correlated instruments. Requires IV percentile tracking and term structure analysis.

## Earnings Event Structures (TBS #021–#030)
Iron condors or straddles sized around expected move. Enter before earnings, exit same day or next session.

## Skew Dislocation (TBS #031–#040)
Exploit put/call skew mispricings using risk reversals or skew-relative strikes on spreads.

## Theta Harvest / Premium Selling (TBS #041–#050)
Sell defined-risk credit spreads, iron condors, or iron flies in high-IV environments (IV rank > 50). Strictly manage max loss at entry.

## Gamma Scalping (TBS #051–#060)
Long gamma positions (straddles/strangles) with dynamic delta hedging. Profitable when realized vol exceeds implied vol paid.

## Calendar & Diagonal Structures (TBS #061–#070)
Exploit term structure slope and time decay differentials between near and far expirations.

## Iron Condors & Butterflies (TBS #071–#080)
Range-bound premium collection. Requires low expected-move environments and careful wing placement.

## Ratio Spreads & Backspreads (TBS #081–#090)
Asymmetric positioning for tail-move capture or income with embedded long exposure.

## Tail Risk / Macro Positioning (TBS #091–#100)
Low-cost far-OTM options for portfolio hedging or macro event trades. High risk/reward, low win rate.
